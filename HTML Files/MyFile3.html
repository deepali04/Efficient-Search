Tom Riddle's History

Early Life:

Tom Marvolo Riddle was born to Tom Riddle Sr. and Merope Gaunt. His mother used a love potion to ensnare his father.

Orphaned at Hogwarts:

After his father abandoned the family, Tom was left at Wool's Orphanage. He discovered his magical abilities at a young age.

Years at Hogwarts:

Tom attended Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry, where he was sorted into Slytherin House. He was a gifted student but showed early signs of dark magic.

Horcruxes and Dark Arts:

Tom delved into the Dark Arts, seeking immortality. He created multiple Horcruxes, splitting his soul and committing heinous acts to achieve his goals.

Rise to Power:

Assuming the name Lord Voldemort, Tom Riddle rose to power as the Dark Lord. He led the Death Eaters in a quest for pure-blood wizard dominance.

The Prophecy and Downfall:

A prophecy foretold the downfall of Voldemort at the hands of a child. Harry Potter, the chosen one, ultimately defeated him, leading to Voldemort's first downfall.

Resurrection:

Voldemort returned to power years later, seeking to conquer the wizarding world. The Second Wizarding War ensued, culminating in the Battle of Hogwarts.

Final Defeat:

Harry Potter and his allies faced Voldemort in the Battle of Hogwarts. In the end, Voldemort was defeated once and for all by the rebounding Killing Curse.
